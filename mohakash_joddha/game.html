Controls

UP ARROW : move up side

DOWN ARROW : move down side

LEFT ARROW : move left side

RIGHT ARROW : move right side

SPACE BAR : to shoot enemies

Game Rules

Clear all enemies to reach next stage. Try to get energies which bring out after each enemy's destroyed, it'll increase your health. Whenever you fill your health bar your plane will be an upgrade, bullet power also increase help to destroy enemies easily. Interval of next bullet fire depends on your health status, if your health bar 10% it'll take a bit time to next shoot, but if your health is 90% next shoot will take less interval. There also a couple of special powers. few of them increase your movement speed, bullet speed, protection and some of them reduce your movement speed, bullet speed, protection. The special powers are least only for 10 seconds. Now it's time to play the game, hope you'll enjoy a lot.

This game is 90% responsive, can fit any stander size device. I have made a very basic game engine using javascript who just controling the game FPS (Frame Per Seconds). This is a 24 FPS high graphics game for normal processor can perfrom faster.
